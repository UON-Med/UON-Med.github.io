Plan film AXR is usually the first step. Erect or supine. Occasionally decubitus. An AXR is done with suspected bowel obstruction, perforation, foreign body, undifferentiated abdominal pain, or for a renal tract calculus follow-up.

An uneven diaphragm can indicate a perforation from diverticular disease or a peptic ulcer.  

 

Football sign indicates pneumoperitoneum. Note liver ligament indicating air on both sides.
